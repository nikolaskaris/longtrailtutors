Long Trail Tutors

Standardized Test Prep and Academic Tutoring in Vermont

Improve standardized test scores through targeted, personal sessions. Have our tutors guide you through the college application process, or get help on your academic coursework.

For standardized exams, success can be as much about understanding the test as about finding the right answer. Long Trail Tutors works with families to help select standardized exams most suited to students' skills and abilities. We then train our students to think like the tests they are taking. Our strategies enable them to approach each section of their exams with confidence, to unravel questions deliberately, and to select the best answers efficiently.

We offer proprietary standardized test preparation for the following exams:

  • SAT
  • SAT II Subject Tests
  • ACT
  • PSAT
  • SSAT

We select the finest teachers, all of whom are recent graduates of Ivy League and other selective universities with previous tutoring experience. Every one has a compelling background and varied interests.
Founder: Nik Laskaris

Nik grew up in the Mad River Valley, graduating from Harwood Union High School in 2011.
